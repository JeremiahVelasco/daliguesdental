Welcome to our practice conveniently located in the heart of Union Square, San Francisco where you will experience a friendly and caring environment focused on preventive care and education

Our focus is your comfort and well-being. Dr. Daligues has extensive experience in restorative and cosmetic dentistry as well as Invisalign to create the winning smile you deserve.

Invisalign

Invisalign is a popular treatment that uses clear, removable aligners to straighten teeth. It offers a discreet and comfortable alternative to traditional metal braces, allowing individuals to achieve a confident smile without the visible appearance of wires and brackets.

Preventive Dentistry

Our goal is to prevent oral health issues from emerging.

Restorative Dentistry

Our goal is to restore the function and health of your teeth and give you a beautiful smile through different procedures.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Our goal is to enhance your smile and bring out the natural beauty in your teeth. We will utilize veneers and whitening to polish and fix the imperfections in your smile.
