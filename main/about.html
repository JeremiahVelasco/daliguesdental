About

Caroline C. Daligues, D.M.D.


Dr. Caroline Daligues began her practice in Union Square, San Francisco where she's been since 1998. She is a member of the American Dental Association, California Dental Association and San Francisco Dental Society. She has been serving patients in the San Francisco are for the last 18 years. Her patients admire her thoroughness and skills. She is committed to continuing education for her to stay current and updated on procedures and technology so she can provide the best dental care her patients deserve. Her goal is to preserve your natural teeth, alleviate oral discomfort and improve the appearance of your smile. She believes that the smile is a reflection of the overall health and well being of an individual. She gives importance to preventive care because this is the foundation of the overall health of the oral cavity. If you want to feel good, stay healthy and look great throughout life, you might be surprised what a difference healthy teeth can make.
