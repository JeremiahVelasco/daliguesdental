PREVENTIVE DENTISTRY

X-rays

Dental X-rays/radiographs provide accurate images that convey important information about one's oral health. Dentists are able to examine the jaw bone, teeth that have not yet erupted, roots of teeth, and contact areas. X-rays help detect oral health conditions such as cavities, decay, cysts, signs of periodontal disease, and more. They are essential for proper oral care and maintenance and help detect symptoms of diseases and health problems at their early stages.

Sealants

Sealants are thin, protective coatings applied to the chewing surfaces of teeth to prevent tooth decay.
