    How Does Invisalign Work?


    Invisalign uses a series of aligners that will straighten your teeth. The doctor will complete a scan using the iTero 3D scanner to accurately map out a treatment plan. Invisalign aligners will gradually transform your smile comfortably and predictably. Once treatment is finished, you'll see that your teeth will be straighter, your gums healthier, and a more beautiful smile. Invisalign treatment successfully treats patients with moderate and severe conditions, like crowding, spacing, overbite, underbite, deep bite, and open bite. Furthermore, Invisalign treatment does not interrupt your lifestyle since they are removable, meaning you can enjoy the foods and activities you love. Average treatment time is about a year and a visit every month or two is needed to check your progression.
