    • Cosmetic Services

    Veneers

    Thin coverings custom made from porcelain to fit precisely over existing teeth and will be bonded to the teeth permanently. Porcelain laminate veneers will enhance your smile if your teeth are stained, broken, or misshaped. They are extremely strong and durable, taking several years for a potential replacement. For the first visit, a mold is required and temporary veneers are necessary to protect the teeth. The second appointment will be the cementation and will be followed by polishing for the veneers to blend with the rest of the teeth.

    Whitening

    This procedure begins with the preparation to protect the gums and the lips leaving only the teeth exposed. Hydrogen peroxide, the bleaching agent, will be applied to the teeth and with the use of a special light, they will gently penetrate the teeth, breaking up stains and discolorations. It is safe, effective, and fast. In less than an hour your teeth will become whiter immediately.
