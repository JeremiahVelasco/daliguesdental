Preventive Dentistry

Preventive dentistry focuses on proactive care, including regular check-ups, cleanings, and education on maintaining good oral hygiene habits. By preventing dental problems early, you can enjoy a beautiful smile for years to come.

Restorative Dentistry

Restorative dentistry is the art of repairing and renewing your smile. From fillings to crowns and implants, it's about restoring teeth to their optimal function and appearance, ensuring your confidence and comfort.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry enhances the aesthetics of your smile. It includes procedures like teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers to help you achieve the dazzling, confident smile you've always wanted.

Invisalign

Invisalign is a popular treatment that uses clear, removable aligners to straighten teeth. It offers a discreet and comfortable alternative to traditional metal braces, allowing individuals to achieve a confident smile without the visible appearance of wires and brackets.

  • It is removable so you can brush and floss normally which will prevent tooth decay and gum disease during orthodontic treatment. You can also eat and drink what you want while in treatment.
  • It is invisible- people can hardly tell that you are straightening your teeth. You can actually smile more during treatment.
  • It is comfortable because there is no metal or wires to cause gum irritations during treatment.
  • We use the iTero 3D scanner to diagnose malocclusion and record accurate digital models of our patients' teeth.
